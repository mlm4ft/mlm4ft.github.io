About me

The possession of knowledge does not kill the sense of wonder and mystery. There is always more mystery.

I am in the final semester of my program, and will graduate in May with a Master’s of Library and Information Science. My work for the MLIS has focused largely on systems administration and electronic resources. But I’ve also been very interested in the theoretical aspects of library work. I’ve spent a lot of time thinking about how libraries anchor their communities. I hope to continue pursuing that question throughout my career.
